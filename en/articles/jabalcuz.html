Jabalcuz

Published on 10/06/2013
Jaén / Área metropolitana de Jaén
  • Located 3 Kilometres away from the city and 1614 meters high, it is one of the main features of our landscape and it is in the heart of Jaen’s people.
  • The first documents about its hot springs dates back to 1594, being the thermal bath built at the beginning of the 17th century.
  • Unfortunately abandoned today, the baths were at their best at the end of the 19th century and beginning of the 20th century.
  • The gardens, of a great landscape and botanical value, offers to the visitor a space for relaxing, enhancing the contact with nature.
  • Jabalcuz’s baths and gardens were declared of cultural interest in 2009.
