The Snails

Published on 11/05/2015
Jimena / Sierra Magina
  • Around 3 kms away from Jimena, “Los Caracoles’ is one of the most amazing corners inside Sierra Magina Natural Park.
  • We will find a few little huts made of stone and raised from rubble.
  • The stones are not joined together using any mortar, it’s built using the ancient building tradition of ‘dry stone’.
  • Built with hardly any tool and without transport, these huts were raised stone by stone by stone masons that used to work and spend long seasons under very hard living conditions.
  • This kind of architecture is often related to the old Celtic buildings in Asturias and Galicia.
