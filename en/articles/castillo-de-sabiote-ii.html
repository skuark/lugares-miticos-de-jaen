Castillo de Sabiote II

Published on 26/10/2015
Sabiote (La Loma y Las Villas)
Monuments, Castles and Fortresses
  • Influenced by Renaissance Italian fortresses,it is the oldest example of Renaissance castle fortified with bastions preserved in the whole country.
  • The inside was severely damaged by lootings and bombing by Napoleon’s troops.
  • However, the restoration works that took place provide visitors with a very good idea of the majesty of the most important military construction in the province during the 16th century.
  • Declared Artistic Monument in 1931, in this second issue we will focus on the inside.
