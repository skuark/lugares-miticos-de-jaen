La Aliseda

Published on 15/02/2016
Santa Elena (Sierra Norte)
Nature, Despeñaperros Natural Park
  • La Aliseda is located next to the mount under the same name, in an area that for centuries as been very well known by its landscapes and medicinal waters.
  • It is the perfect place to enjoy a picnic in an outstanding natural environment, it has tables, bbqs and play zones for the little ones.
  • The Rio Campana path starts right next to it. It is a brief but remarkable round route of less than 1 kilometre long and low difficulty.
  • In our way we will find oak trees, ash trees, alder trees, and a group of huge stone pines. We will enjoy the leafy wood that grows next to the river.
  • Autumn and spring would be the best seasons to do this route, because the river has more water during this time. This walk is also a reference for mushrooms collectors.
