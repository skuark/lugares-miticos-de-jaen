Baños Árabes

Published on 05/10/2015
Segura de la Sierra (Sierra De Segura)
Monuments, Sierras de Cazorla, Segura y Las Villas Natural Park
  • Situated in the middle of an urban area in Segura de la Sierra, there are one of the few Arabic baths still preserved in the province.
  • To visit them we will have to go down Calle de la Iglesia, finding the entrance in Calle Caballeros Santiaguistas.
  • They are made by 3 naves linked to the typical muslim baths rooms: the cold room -outside one- the warm room and the hot room -inner one-.
  • After being used as a residence, they were restored in 1971.
  • The origin is probably in the 11th and 12th centuries, a flourishing time in this beautiful place.
