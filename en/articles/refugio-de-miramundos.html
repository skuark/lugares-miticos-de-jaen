Refugio de Miramundos

Published on 21/02/2016
Albanchez de Mágina, Huelma / Sierra Magina
  • Miramundos’ shelter is located at 2077 meters high and offers spectacular views over Jaen’s province and beyond.
  • From Miramundos we could see Sierras de Cazorla, Segura y Las Villas Park, Sierra de Andujar and other mountain ranges outside Jaen such as, Sierra de Baza, Sierra Nevada or Sierras de Tejeda, Almijara and Alhama’s Natural Park.
  • Part of the path we take to go to Miramundos is shared with the path to go to Magina’s Peak – with 2167 meters high is the ceiling of Jaen’s Province – so this route is not an easy one due to the high incline level.
  • The shelter has been refurbished lately by the Mountain Club ‘Amigos de Miramundos’. Currently it is in good conditions, and it holds an annual visitors book collecting all visitors’ comments.
