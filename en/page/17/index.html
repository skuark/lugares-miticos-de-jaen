El Turronero

Published on 24/06/2013
Jaén / Área metropolitana de Jaén

El Turronero es otro de negocios familiares con historia, una tienda con todo tipo de caramelos y golosinas que hacen las delicias de los más pequeños desde hace muchos años. Las piñatas de los cumpleaños, los mantecados y turrones de navidad, las bolsitas de chucherías que encargan los reyes magos para repartirlas por los hogares de Jaén y esas grúas metálicas de juguete que han acompañado a varias generaciones. Mimo Libros

Jaén / Área metropolitana de Jaén

Mimo Libros is an old school book shop, it looks more like a library than a simple bookshop. The magic of literature wraps the visitors from the very moment they step in, the paper’s scent, the high shelves full of books and an atmosphere that invites to relax and chill, a place where the clock stops and the rush is scared away. Jabalcuz

Published on 10/06/2013
Jaén / Área metropolitana de Jaén

When we talk about Jabalcuz, we are mentioning a reference for everybody in Jaen, it’s not only for its impressive presence in the landscape, but also due to its relevance in history. Its hot spring was the origin of the building of a thermal and touristic center together with amazing gardens and a wonderful natural environment, it makes an ideal place for enjoying strolls and spare time. Jaén / Área metropolitana de Jaén
