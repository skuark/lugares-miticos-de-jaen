El Encinarejo

Published on 06/06/2016
Andújar (Campiña)
Nature, Sierra de Andujar Natural Park

Sierra de Andujar Natural Park is specially well known for being the last place where Iberian lynx can still be found. However, beyond this amazing fact that makes it different from any other park, we need to add the stunning mountain ranges, a luxury to the senses. The area we are approaching today, El Encinarejo, is considered as the main entrance to the park, and the path that runs through it is one of the busiest. The river Jandula makes alive a beautiful riverside wood, and walking through it we will see the wide variety of trees and animals that live here. We will finish our walk at the dam of Encinarejo, a little reservoir used in the production of electric energy. Read full article »

Santuario de la Virgen de la Cabeza

Published on 18/04/2016
Andújar (Campiña)
Nature, Monuments, Sierra de Andujar Natural Park

Virgen de la Cabeza’s Shrine during the last week of April is the destination of thousands of pilgrims in one of the oldest pilgrimages in Spain. The last Sunday of April the procession of the Virgen de la Cabeza - known as “Morenita”- is celebrated here since 1505, being this shrine the centre of the event. Cofraidias (religious brotherhoods) from all over the country, decorated with flags and coats of arms all the path from Andujar till the Shrine, where they go to honour the image of the Virgin. Embalse del Jándula

Published on 21/04/2014
Andújar (Campiña)
Nature, Sierra de Andujar Natural Park

El Parque Natural de la Sierra de Andújar está situado al noroeste de al provincia de Jaén, en pleno corazón de Sierra Morena. El río Jándula y sus dos embalses, conforman uno de los paisajes más espectaculares del parque. En el recorrido hacia el embalse del Jándula nos encontraremos con restos de edificaciones del poblado de La Lancha, que acogió a los hombres y mujeres que trabajaron en la construcción del embalse. Zona de un inmenso valor ecológico y paisajístico, el Parque Natural de la Sierra de Andújar destaca por su fauna, donde el protagonista indiscutible es el lince ibérico. Read full article »
