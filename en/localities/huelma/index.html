Refugio de Miramundos

Published on 21/02/2016
Albanchez de Mágina (Sierra Magina) , Huelma (Sierra Magina)
Nature, Sierra Mágina Natural Park

Sierra Magina Natural Park holds the highest peaks in the province. Miramundos (meaning ‘look at the world)’ shelter is 2077 meters high, and it offers spectacular views of Jaen’s province and beyond; allowing us to see Sierra Nevada, Sierra de Baza or Sierras de Tejada, Almijara and Alhama Natural Park. To get up there we will take the path from Canada de las Cruces; and it is a hard route as part of it is shared with the path to Magina’s Peak. After going through a high incline level we will finally arrive to Miramundos; our reward, not just because of the beauty of the path we followed to come here, but also the remarkable sight to our eyes when we get to the shelter, pure luxury to our senses.
