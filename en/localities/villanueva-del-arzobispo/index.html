Villanueva del Arzobispo (La Loma y Las Villas)
Monuments, Sierras de Cazorla, Segura y Las Villas Natural Park

In the year 1396 Villanueva del Arzobispo splitted from Iznatoraf, becoming a complete independent Villa. La Moraleja - its previous name - held the Virgen de la Fuensanta’s Sanctuary, well known at the time as a pilgrimage place. The temple, built on site of a previous castle of which two towers remains, has been managed by different religious orders but the Trinitarians are the current residents.
