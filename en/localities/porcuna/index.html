Cerrillo Blanco

Published on 03/11/2015
Porcuna (Área metropolitana de Jaén)
Monuments, Iberians

Located approximately 3 Kms away from Porcuna, Cerrillo Blanco is one of the most important archeological sites from the Iberian times, not just in Jaen’s province, but also in the whole peninsula. At the archeological site we can visit the necropolis of burials from the 7th century BC - made up by 24 single burials pits and one megalithic burial - which was still in use till the 1st century BC when the Roman burial style was adopted. The other big discovery of the archeological site, the group of Iberian sculptures made by more than 50 high quality sculptures, can be seen both at the Museo de Porcuna and at the Museo Provincial de Jaen.
