Rus (La Loma y Las Villas)
Monuments

6 Kilometres away from Rus, it is found one of the most unknown treasures in the province of Jaen. This shrine of visigothic origins - unique in the south of Spain- dates from the 6th and 7th centuries. The site is made of a main cave and two other ones next to it, all of them carved directly on the rock.The main cave - which preserves the beautiful facade directly carved on the terrain slope- was used as a chapel, while the other two were used for baptism and monk’s residence.
