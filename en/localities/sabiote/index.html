Sabiote Castle I

Published on 21/09/2015
Sabiote (La Loma y Las Villas)
Monuments, Castles and Fortresses

It holds a place of huge importance due to its strategic position. Sabiote had always have military fortresses throughout history. A pre-Roman oppidium, a Musulman fortress and a Calatravan castle are the predecessors of the spectacular Renaissance fortress built in the 16th century by Francisco de Cobos, who commissioned Pedro de Vandelvira. Strongly influenced by Renaissance Italian fortresses, he built the oldest example of Renaissance castle fortified with bastions preserved, (in outstanding conditions)in the whole country.
