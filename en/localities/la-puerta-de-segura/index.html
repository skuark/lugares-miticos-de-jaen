La Puerta de Segura (Sierra De Segura)
Nature, Sierras de Cazorla, Segura y Las Villas Natural Park

Natural entrance to Sierra de Segura, La Puerta de Segura is a compulsory way in order to access a big number of its towns and villages. It welcomes us with a beautiful path near the Guadalimar river and its clear water that divides the town in two. La Puerta is surrounded by a remarkable natural environment. Read full article »
